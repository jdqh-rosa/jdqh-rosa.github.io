The price of exploring space

This was an 8 week long project for school where I had to work in a team to produce a polished game to be used as an interactive exhibit in a museum. We decided to create a game that focused on managing the debris that gets left orbiting our planet as a result of space missions.

As the only programmer in the team I did all the programming for this game. The game itself wasn't all that complicated to make, mostly involving various orbiting objects, some power-ups and tooling for the designers. At the start of the project we were planning to make several levels where the player would have to navigate a series of rocket launches through clouds of space trash. Most of my time in the first early weeks was spent on making a level editor before we decided to scrap the levels.

Synopsis:

For far too long has humanity only dreamt about exploring the galaxy. Now it is time to wake up and realize that dream. But beware, with every rocket you send up a cloud of debris gets left behind. Balance our thirst for exploration with the need to keep our skies clear.
