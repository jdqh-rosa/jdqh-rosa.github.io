Thor: Gone to Hel

  1. Home
  2. Thor: Gone to Hel

Project Information

  • Category: Video Game
  • Client: School Project
  • Project date: 2019
  • Project URL: Visit Website

Classic arcade hammer throwing

This was a 2 week long group project where we had to make a "retro" arcade game inside the GXPEngine (a proprietary engine provided by the school). I worked with another programmer in this group and we split the implementation of the game evenly between us. I also made the controller that would be used to play the game using an Arduino. The controller is made from a potmeter and a button connected to an Arduino.

Synopsis:

Asgard is being attacked by the army of the undead. The god of thunder, Thor, went to Helheim to find Hel, the queen of the undead, and make her stop this invasion. After arriving in Helheim and fighting against more undead he will reach the throne room in which Hel will be.
